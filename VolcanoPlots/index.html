Volcano Plot

  • Zoom, selection tools, and axis reset are available in the top-right corner.

  • Green points indicate significant up-regulation.

  • Yellow points (there are 50) highlight areas of interest, typically located in the top corners.

  • Red points indicate significant down-regulation (Â±0.5 log2 fold change, p-value < 0.05).

  • Blue points represent all other data points.

Colored Cells

  • Each cell shows p-value / adj. p-value.

  • Green cells: both p-values under 0.05.

  • Yellow cells: p-value under 0.05, but adj. p-value above 0.05.

  • Red cells: both p-values above 0.05.

  • If no compound identifier exists, Calc. MW will be shown.

  • Use "Show/Hide Additional Columns" to toggle the "Calc. MW" and "m/z" columns.
